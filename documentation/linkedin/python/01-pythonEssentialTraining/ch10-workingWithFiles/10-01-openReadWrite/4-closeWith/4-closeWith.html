close with

Using with will allow us to close a file within the scope of the with block. 

f = open('newFile.txt','w')
f.write('Line 1\n')
f.write('Line 2\n')
with open('newFile.txt','a') as f:
    f.write('Another line\n')
    f.write('Another Line again\n')

 We still having access to it outside of the block But, we cannot modify the file outside of the block
