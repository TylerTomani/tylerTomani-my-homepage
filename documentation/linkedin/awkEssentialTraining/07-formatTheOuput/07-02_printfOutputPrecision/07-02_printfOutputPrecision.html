Change precision

 The above printf has an integer %d placeholder. Change this to %f.

 Now we can adjust the precision. The number after the . limits the decimal to that place. The number before the decimal is the entire width.

 6.2 will have 6 numbers all together and 2 digits after the decimal.

awk -F , '{printf("%-20s\t %-32s\t %6.2 f\n",$1,$2,$3)}'
